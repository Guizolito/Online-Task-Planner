What is this?

First and foremost, welcome to our page. This website is intendes to help out the less organized ones. Tired of forgetting your agenda at home? Well with this webpage, you will be able to have it with you at all times. From reminders to alarms, you'll be able to carefully align your future events.

Why?

As a very clumsy person at times, i believe that only using your memory was at most times not enough to remind yourself of future events. What better way to fix that than making your own online agenda.
